Restaurant Options

​The Heights neighborhood in Houston offers a vibrant culinary scene with numerous hip and popular restaurants catering to various tastes throughout the day. Here's a curated list of notable spots for breakfast, lunch, and dinner

Breakfast

  • Snooze, an A.M. Eatery: Renowned for its creative breakfast dishes and energetic atmosphere, Snooze offers a modern twist on classic morning fare. ​
  • Lola: A favorite among locals, Lola serves hearty American breakfasts in a cozy setting, making it an ideal spot to start your day. ​
  • Dandelion Cafe: This café emphasizes fresh, locally sourced ingredients, providing a bright and welcoming environment for breakfast enthusiasts.

Lunch

  • Field & Tides: Offering a menu that blends Southern comfort with contemporary flair, Field & Tides is a popular lunch destination in The Heights. ​
  • Better Luck Tomorrow: Known for its innovative dishes and laid-back ambiance, this spot is perfect for a casual yet delectable lunch. ​
  • Handies Douzo: Specializing in fresh hand rolls and sushi, Handies Douzo provides a unique lunch experience for sushi aficionados. ​

Dinner

  • Jūn: A recent addition to The Heights, Jūn has quickly gained acclaim for its inventive menu, earning a semifinalist spot in the Best New Restaurant category of the James Beard Foundation Awards in 2024.​
  • Squable: Celebrated for its European-inspired dishes and chic atmosphere, Squable offers a memorable dining experience for dinner guests. ​​
  • La Lucha: Combining Southern flavors with Gulf Coast influences, La Lucha provides a lively setting and a menu that delights dinner patrons. ​ ​
  • Each of these establishments captures the essence of The Heights' dynamic food scene, ensuring a delightful culinary experience no matter the time of day.​
